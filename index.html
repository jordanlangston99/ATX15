Who We Are Supporting

The Center for Child Protection is the only nonprofit in Travis County involved in the investigation of crimes against children. All services are provided completely FREE in English and Spanish.

Our vision is to end the cycle of child abuse through advocacy, education, and community support. A forensic interview is the first step for children in the intervention process. The Center offers a wide-range of unique therapy experiences. Free prevention training is available to the Austin community.
